P4: Global circuit breaker — aggregate portfolio P&L gate

## Parent
Tracked in #206 (P4)

## Problem

No drawdown protection across the portfolio. If multiple pairs blow up simultaneously (correlated regime break), there's no kill switch. Individual pair stop-losses don't protect against portfolio-level tail risk.

## Fix

Add a portfolio-level circuit breaker to PairsEngine:
- Track aggregate unrealized + realized P&L across all active pairs
- If daily P&L drawdown exceeds threshold (e.g., -2% of portfolio), flatten all positions and halt new entries
- Resume next session (or after configurable cooldown)
- Threshold configurable in `config/pairs.toml`

## Evidence

- Standard institutional risk management — per-desk daily loss limits
- Our PNC/USB + HD/LOW losses (-$580) happened on the same day — a circuit breaker would have capped the damage

## Files
- `engine/crates/core/src/pairs/engine.rs` — add P&L tracking + halt logic
- `config/pairs.toml` — `max_daily_loss_pct` parameter
